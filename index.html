"Dear Friend —"

I think in all fairness, I think I should tell you exactly what it is that I do.

Let me be crystal clear — so there's no confusion.

Because the way you "got her" was wrong from the start. The dynamic was off. The roles were never reversed. She was still the prize — and you were the chaser.

You were living in a #2 relationship where you had the #1 feeling for her, but she only had the #2 feeling for you.

And that's not good enough.

That's why you need me.

Because no one on this planet is better at helping a man get the woman he's crazy about — and flipping the dynamic so she feels like he's the prize. That's the world's greatest magic trick.

Sound easy? It's not.

Not on a normal day. And it's 100x harder when you're head over heels for her. That's why trying to pull this off without coaching is almost impossible.

I love this job. I love my clients. But I'll be honest — sometimes I get frustrated.

Because men suffer in silence. They don't talk. They don't ask for help. They just bury the pain and disappear.

They write me messages like: "Chris, I think I'm losing the love of my life."

But we both know what happened. She dumped him. He's heartbroken. Angry. Bitter. And he has no idea how to fix it.

Worse? He'll repeat the exact same pattern with the next woman. Because he never took the time to learn what went wrong.
